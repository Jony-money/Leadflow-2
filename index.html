📨 Hostinger Email (via Brevo)Not configured
How it works: Browsers can't send SMTP directly, so we relay through Brevo (free: 300 emails/day ≈ 9,000/month). Your "From" address is your Hostinger email — recipients reply to your Hostinger inbox normally.
📋 Setup steps (click to expand)
  1. Sign up free at brevo.com (no credit card needed).
  2. Get your API key: top-right menu → SMTP & API → API Keys → Generate a new API key. Copy it (starts with xkeysib-).
  3. Verify your sender so Brevo will send from your Hostinger address: Senders, Domains & Dedicated IPs → Senders → Add a sender. Enter your Hostinger email, Brevo emails you a confirmation link — open it from Hostinger webmail and click confirm.
  4. Paste the API key + your sender email into the fields below, enable, and hit Send Test Email.
  5. Optional but strongly recommended for deliverability: Add Brevo's SPF + DKIM DNS records to your Hostinger domain (Brevo's Domains page shows the exact records). Without these, emails may land in spam. Takes 5 min in hPanel → Domains → DNS Zone Editor.
⚠ Note: Reply auto-tracking only works for Gmail. Hostinger replies arrive in your Hostinger webmail — you mark them in the app manually.
